school library

was built in 1980

A school library (or a school library media center) is a library within a school where students, staff, and often, parents of a public or private school have access to a variety of resources.

school auditorium

was built in 1982

A school auditorium is a hallowed place for the congregation of the entire populace of the school for commemorative or solemn occasions. Usually a spacious hall with Corinthian columns, a stage or dias for the head of school to address the students, large seating arrangement to accommodate as many students as possible.
